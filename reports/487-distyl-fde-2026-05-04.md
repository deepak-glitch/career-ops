**Why are you interested in Distyl AI?**
> Distyl is one of the few enterprise AI companies actually deploying compound AI workflows into customer-critical operations — telecom, healthcare, insurance, manufacturing — and proving it with a 100% production-deployment rate and a profitable business. My 2.5 years at Progress Solutions has been exactly that work in healthcare: building multi-agent claims pipelines with schema-validated JSON contracts, RAG with ~35% retrieval-precision gains, agentic LLM workflows with >30% hallucination reduction, and FastAPI/Docker packaging that cut post-deployment defects ~30%. I want to do this work at Distyl's scale and across its customer breadth.

**Tell us about an AI system you've shipped end-to-end.**
> I built a multi-agent healthcare claims processing pipeline — Intake Normalization → Validation → Consistency Analysis → Duplicate Detection → Fraud Risk Scoring. The key design decision was schema-validated JSON contracts between agents to prevent cascading hallucinations. RAG-grounded CPT/ICD validation against vector-indexed policy docs handles validation; ANN similarity catches duplicates; explainable risk scoring with audit-ready reasoning traces makes the output reviewable. Each agent is bounded — it has a contract for what it consumes and what it emits — and that's what makes the system production-ready instead of a brittle chain-of-thought.
