## H) Draft Application Answers (score 3.6 — included for ready-to-apply)

**Why are you interested in Extend?**
> Extend is doing the LLM-document-processing work I've been building in healthcare — extract structured signal from unstructured documents, ground it, validate it, and ship it into customer workflows. At Progress Solutions I've built a multi-agent healthcare claims pipeline (Intake → Validation → Consistency → Duplicate → Fraud Risk Scoring) with schema-validated JSON contracts between agents to prevent cascading hallucinations, and a healthcare RAG system with ~35% retrieval-precision gains and >90% grounded alignment. I want to do this work at the scale of Brex, Checkr, and Opendoor, with the customer-side ownership Extend's FDE role asks for.

**Tell us about a customer-environment problem you owned end-to-end.**
> At Progress Solutions, the EHR data feeding our care-engagement scoring model was inconsistent — appointment histories had encoding errors, support ticket logs had inconsistent labels. I built preprocessing pipelines (Pandas, NumPy) for EHR extracts, raised dataset reliability above 98%, and cut downstream model instability ~40%. But the deeper problem was clinician trust: the model had to come with stakeholder-facing system-limitation docs and audit trails. So I authored the limitation docs, set up evaluation audit trails, and walked clinicians through what the model couldn't answer. That's the part of the FDE work most engineers skip — and it's the part that actually unlocks adoption.
