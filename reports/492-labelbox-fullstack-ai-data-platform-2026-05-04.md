## H) Draft Application Answers (score 3.5 — included for ready-to-apply)

**Why are you interested in Labelbox?**
> Labelbox sits at exactly the layer I care about: the infrastructure that lets human reviewers + LLMs together produce high-quality training data and evaluation signal for frontier AI. My production work at Progress Solutions has been eval-first Applied AI in healthcare — RAG with ~35% retrieval precision and >90% groundedness, agentic LLM workflows with schema-validated JSON contracts between agents (>30% hallucination reduction), and explainable risk scoring with audit-ready reasoning traces. At Suvidha I built a video-summarization system whose primary success metric was ~85% alignment between AI-selected highlights and human-curated key moments — that's exactly the HITL comparison loop Labelbox builds tooling for. I want to bring that eval-first + HITL discipline to the platform layer.

**Tell us about an AI evaluation system you've built.**
> The Agentic Healthcare Claims Processing pipeline. Five agents — Intake Normalization → Validation → Consistency Analysis → Duplicate Detection → Fraud Risk Scoring. The key design decision was schema-validated JSON contracts between agents to prevent cascading hallucinations: each agent has a contract for what it consumes and what it emits. Validation is RAG-grounded against vector-indexed CPT/ICD policies. Duplicate detection uses ANN similarity. Risk scoring outputs explainable reasoning traces — a human reviewer can step through why a claim got flagged, which is the whole point: the system isn't asking "trust me," it's saying "here's why, audit it." That ability to expose reasoning + ground in policy is what makes it ready substrate for LLM-in-loop review and human approval workflows.
