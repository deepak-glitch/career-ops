## H) Draft Application Answers

**Q: Why Notion?**
> Notion is the rare consumer SaaS where AI is woven into how millions of people structure their daily work — not bolted on. The three team tracks listed (AI product, model & systems, evals) map directly to what I've been doing for the last 2.5 years on healthcare RAG and agents at Progress Solutions: ship the feature, then harden it with multi-provider routing and grounded-response evaluation gates. I want to do that on a product that millions love.

**Q: A time you shipped something users felt:**
> I shipped Manga Lens to the Chrome Web Store as a solo project — a real-time AI translation extension for manga / webtoon panels. Manifest V3 service workers, four AI vision providers (Claude Sonnet, GPT-4o-mini, GPT-4.1-Nano, local Ollama) with per-provider failure isolation, and per-domain selectors for 29 sites. The trickiest part was payload routing: cloud providers want WebP, but Ollama crashes on WebP, so JPEG-by-route. It taught me that user experience in an AI product is mostly graceful degradation, not raw model quality.

**Q: A time you built something with feedback / collaboration:**
> At Progress Solutions, I built a healthcare RAG system that hit ~35% retrieval precision improvement and >90% grounded-response alignment. The wins came from collaborating with clinical stakeholders on what "grounded" actually meant — chunking, embeddings, and grounding rules all changed once we wrote stakeholder-facing limitation docs. Documentation is design.

**Q: Anything else?**
> I'm authorized on F-1 OPT (STEM extension active through 2027). Future H-1B sponsorship will be required, which Notion has a clear track record on per public USCIS filings.
