> The 70/30 split — customer-facing onboarding, demos, POCs, expansion — is what I've been doing implicitly already. **Walk me through a POC you built that closed a deal (or unblocked a customer).**
> Dream Decoder. The naive direct-prompt orchestration was failing first-pass on image generation — alignment between user dream → poetic interpretation → Replicate image was inconsistent, and we were burning generations on misses. I introduced an intermediate structured-prompt transformation layer between user input and Replicate/Perplexity Sonar APIs: extract semantic anchors, normalize tone, project into a 16:9-friendly visual scaffold. Result: ~30% alignment lift and ~25-30% first-pass image success rate improvement. The lesson I'd carry into Firecrawl: for an API/devtools product, the POC isn't the bullet point — it's the proof that the customer's end-state actually works. Build the transformation layer that makes their downstream LLM calls behave.

For SF Hybrid Full-Time I'm open to relocation; for Remote Americas Contract I overlap fully with both PT and ET working hours.
